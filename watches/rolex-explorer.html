FAQ

What is the best Explorer homage?
By our rubric the closest is the SN017 from San Martin (fidelity 86). On a budget, the PD-1693 at about $90 gets you most of the look for the least money.

Are Explorer homages legal?
Yes. An homage borrows design language — proportions, dial layout, bezel style — without carrying another brand's name or logo. That is legal and openly traded; Timex, Bulova and Steinhart all sell homages. We list only homages here, never counterfeits.

How much can you save versus a Rolex Explorer?
The original is about $7,700. Homages here run from roughly $90 up, so you keep the design cues and lose most of the price.
