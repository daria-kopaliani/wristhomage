FAQ

What is the best Aqua Terra homage?
By our rubric the closest is the SN0113W from San Martin (fidelity 85). On a budget, the Teak Dress 39 at about $185 gets you most of the look for the least money.

Are Aqua Terra homages legal?
Yes. An homage borrows design language — proportions, dial layout, bezel style — without carrying another brand's name or logo. That is legal and openly traded; Timex, Bulova and Steinhart all sell homages. We list only homages here, never counterfeits.

How much can you save versus a Omega Aqua Terra?
The original is about $6,200. Homages here run from roughly $185 up, so you keep the design cues and lose most of the price.
